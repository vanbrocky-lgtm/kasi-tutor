INCOME STATEMENT (FULL NOTES)

Income Statement shows profit or loss.

Structure:
  1. Sales
  2. Cost of Sales
  3. Gross Profit
  4. Other Income
  5. Expenses
  6. Net Profit
Sales: Income from selling goods
Example: R20 000

Cost of Sales: Opening Stock + Purchases – Closing Stock
Example: 5 000 + 15 000 – 4 000 = R16 000

Gross Profit: Sales – Cost of Sales
Example: 20 000 – 16 000 = R4 000

Expenses include:
  • Salaries
  • Rent
  • Electricity
  • Advertising
  • Insurance
  • Depreciation

Depreciation: Cost × Rate
Example: 10 000 × 10% = R1 000

Adjustments:

Accrued Expense → Expense owed
Accrued Income → Income owed to business
Prepaid Expense → Paid early
Income Received in Advance → Not yet earned

Examples:
Accrued Expense: +R1 000
Accrued Income: +R2 000
Prepaid: subtract unused
Income in advance: subtract
